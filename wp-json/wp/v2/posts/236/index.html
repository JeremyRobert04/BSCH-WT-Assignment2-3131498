The draw for the 2024 Olympic Games will take place on Tuesday, March 19 at 7pm at FIBA headquarters. The hats have been unveiled by the international federation, and the French women’s team is in the third hat.<\/p>\n\n\n\n

There are only a few days left to wait before we discover Les Bleues’ program for the big Olympic event (July 26 – August 11).<\/p>\n\n\n\n

France is in the third hat of the four that will make up the draw on Tuesday evening in Switzerland.<\/p>\n\n\n\n

Three groups of four teams will be drawn. The top two from each group and the two best runners-up will then qualify for the quarter-finals. A draw will determine the head-to-head matches.<\/p>\n\n\n\n
